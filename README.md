## 🧠 About Me

Hey there! I'm **Bhavishy Sankhla**, a Computer Science & Engineering undergrad at **LMCST, Jodhpur** — currently in the early phase of my engineering journey, and already building things that matter.

I'm drawn to the intersection of **AI, product thinking, and real-world impact** — especially products that make campus life, learning, and everyday workflows smoother. I don't just study concepts; I turn them into working systems.

My approach: **build → break → learn → repeat.**

- 🎓 B.Tech in CSE @ Lachoo Memorial College of Science & Technology
- 🌍 Based in Jodhpur, Rajasthan, India
- 🔭 Currently exploring **Generative AI**, **LLM applications**, and the **Firebase ecosystem**
- 🛠️ Building tools for students and campus communities
- 🎤 Organizing hackathons & technical events | Passionate about TED-style speaking
- 🌱 Open source learner | Believer in building in public

### ☕ Cafeteria Menu System

**Problem:** Cafeteria ordering at colleges is chaotic — long queues, miscommunication between students and staff, and zero order tracking.

**Solution:** A Firebase-powered ordering platform where students browse the menu, add items to cart, and receive a unique token for pickup — while an admin dashboard gives cafeteria staff real-time order visibility.
